Fresh Caprese

This is a delicious, refreshing, and healthy salad to eat! I love it!

Ingredients

  • Freshly Picked Basil
  • Fresh, Sliced Motzarella Cheese
  • Sliced Tomato
  • balsamic vinagar
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredints in the following order from bottom up: Tomato, Motzarella, Basil
  2. Drizzle some olive oil and balsamic vinagar on top
  3. Enjoy this delicious italian salad!
