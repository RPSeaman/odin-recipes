Yummy Cheese and Crackers

This is a delicious snack to munch on. Super tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of triscuits (any flavor will work but my favorite is black pepper and rosemary
  • 1 wheel of laughing cow spreadable cheese

Steps

  1. Spread the cheese on the crackers
  2. Serve on a large serving plate
  3. Enjoy!
