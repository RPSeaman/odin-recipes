The Clasic Milkshake

This is a delicious vanilla milkshake that my family has made for many many years! Feel free to add you own twists in as well! This recipe makes enough for two people to share!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of your favorite vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix all three ingredients together.
  2. Pour in to 2 large glasses.
  3. Enjoy together with someone special!
